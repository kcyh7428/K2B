Slide Flow & Interactive Elements

How should the presentation move between sections? Click to select.

A

Keyboard-driven slides

Arrow keys or spacebar to advance. Classic presentation feel. You control the pace, audience watches. Simple, no surprises.

B

Scroll-based storytelling

One continuous page that scrolls. Sections fade/slide in as you scroll down. More like a website experience. Unusual for a meeting -- that's the point.

C

Click-to-reveal sections

All sections visible as cards/blocks. Click each to expand. Non-linear -- you can jump to any section based on conversation flow. Most flexible if the Q&A goes in unexpected directions.

Context

Remember: 19 people, projector, 30 minutes, conversational tone. The CV is your opening -- it needs to be front and center. You want flexibility if the room reacts and you need to pivot.
